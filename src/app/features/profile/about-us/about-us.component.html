'SYTM' is a community of sports lovers, who had been gathering together during major sport tournaments over the years, showcasing their talent on predicting match results and other events.

2010. It all started with an excel sheet, couple of curious minds and a need to spice up the FIFA World Cup 2010 among a group of football lovers. Ever since then prediction games have been conducted in various formats for major tournaments. The process became refined through each iterations and the popularity grew over the years.

2024. Pred2win webapp is launched to conduct the SYTM prediction games. This is the beta version of the app, with limited members to perform test on the functionalities during Euro 2024.

Post Euro 2024, the app will be made unavailable temporarily while some upgrades (as observed from the tournament) will be applied. Pred2win is expected to return with more features and enhanced user interface to support more members!
