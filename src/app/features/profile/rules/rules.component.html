 Verification

Building Trust and Harmony

Member Verification is a crucial step to ensure trust and harmony within the SYTM community. It helps to maintain the integrity of the platform and ensures that all members are genuine sports enthusiasts.

Current Verification Process

In the current phase, with a limited number of members, verification is conducted by the Admin through a quick background check. This phase primarily involves existing members, making the process straightforward and efficient.

Please ping on Whatsapp community group or drop a mail to pred2win@gmail.com to get verified.

Future Verification Process

As the community grows, the verification process may evolve to include the uploading of personal identification documents. This will help maintain high standards of security and trust within an expanding member base.

Post-Verification Benefits

Once verified by the Admin, members will receive 1000 SYTM coins. These coins are used to make predictions on polls on pred2win.com.

Prediction Restrictions

Members cannot make any predictions until the verification process is complete. This ensures that all participants in predictions are verified members, fostering a trustworthy environment for everyone.


By adhering to this verification process, we aim to create a more enjoyable platform for all sports lovers. Your cooperation in this process is highly appreciated.

 Matches

The Matches section is divided into four tabs to help you navigate through different stages of match predictions efficiently

Allocated

This tab displays all the matches that have been allocated for predictions.

Matches are allocated 24 hours before their start time, and moved to Ongoing when they start.

Ongoing

This tab shows the matches that are currently ongoing.

Predictions are no longer accepted on polls.

Calculated

This tab contains matches for which the results have been calculated.

Check the polls for the calculated matches to analyze prediction performance and compare results.

Scheduled

This tab lists all the upcoming matches that are scheduled for the future.

Stay organized by knowing the schedule of upcoming matches.


Please note : Matches will not be shown when being calculated.

 Awards

For Champion / Dark Knight/ Fighter Awards, the rank 1, 2, 3 of the leaderboard at the end of the tournament are considered. Coins are credited to respective members once leaderboard is locked for the tournament.

If total predictions, prediction accuracy and bonus accuracy is exactly same for any of the top three members of the leaderboard, the higher award is given to those members.

e.g – Member1 finishes at Champion position and Member2 finishes at Dark Knight position, both predicting 51 matches with accuracy n%, and bonus accuracy with m% (does not depend on total bonus predicted), then both Member1 and Member2 win coins allocated for Champion.

Award nominees for these will be shown on Awards board, after Match 6.


For Predictor Precise, member should predict atleast 75% of the matches in the tournament. Award nominees will be shown after Match 26 (50% of the tournament matches).

In case multiple members are tied for the award, the coins won are split between them.


For Predictor Improbable, only match outcome polls are considered, and not bonus polls. Award nominees are shown from match 2.

In case multiple members are tied for the award, the coins won are split between them.

 Streaks

Coins are credited to member’s account after a match where member has achieved a streak.

Streaks for correct match outcome predictions in a row gets reset if a match is unpredicted.

For Bonus king streak, unpredicted matches are not counted.
