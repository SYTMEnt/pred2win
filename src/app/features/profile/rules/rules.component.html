 Awards

For Champion / Dark Knight/ Fighter Awards, the rank 1, 2, 3 of the leaderboard at the end of the tournament are considered. Coins are credited to respective members once leaderboard is locked for the tournament.

If total predictions, prediction accuracy and bonus accuracy is exactly same for any of the top three members of the leaderboard, the higher award is given to those members.

e.g â€“ Member1 finishes at Champion position and Member2 finishes at Dark Knight position, both predicting 51 matches with accuracy n%, and bonus accuracy with m% (does not depend on total bonus predicted), then both Member1 and Member2 win coins allocated for Champion.

Award nominees for these will be shown on Awards board, after Match 6.


For Predictor Precise, member should predict atleast 75% of the matches in the tournament. Award nominees will be shown after Match 26 (50% of the tournament matches).

In case multiple members are tied for the award, the coins won are split between them.


For Predictor Improbable, only match outcome polls are considered, and not bonus polls. Award nominees are shown from match 2.

In case multiple members are tied for the award, the coins won are split between them.
